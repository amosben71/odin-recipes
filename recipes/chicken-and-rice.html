Shredded Chicken with Rice

This recipe involves a delicious marinated chicken as well
as rice flavored with lime and bay leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken
  • 3 Cups of Rice
  • 2 Limes
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 2 Cups of Olive Oil
  • 5 Cloves of Garlic
  • 5 Tbsp of Chili Powder
  • 2 Tsp of Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste
  • Optional: 1 Broccoli Crown

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 375째F (190째C)
  2. In a large bowl, combine oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and
    pepper. Squeeze the juice of one lime into the bowl and
    mix well to create a marinade.
  3. Add the chicken to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well
    coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to
    allow the flavors to infuse.
  4. While the chicken is marinating, rinse the rice under cold
    water until the water runs clear. This helps to remove excess
    starch and prevents the rice from becoming too sticky.
  5. In a medium saucepan, bring 6 cups of water to a boil. Add the
    bay leaf and a pinch of salt. Stir in the rice, reduce the heat
    to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice
    is tender and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it sit,
    covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork.
  6. While the rice is cooking, place the marinated chicken on a baking
    sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake in the preheated oven for
    25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and has an
    internal temperature of 165째F (74째C). If using broccoli, put it in the
    oven five minutes after the chicken.
  7. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oven and let it rest
    for a few minutes before shredding it with two forks.
  8. Serve the shredded chicken over the cooked rice. Squeeze the juice
    of the remaining lime over the top for added freshness. Enjoy!
